Contributions

MU-LLaMA Model

We introduce the MU-LLaMA model, an exceptional advancement capable of performing music question answering and music captioning tasks

MusicQA Dataset

We propose a systematic approach for creating the music question answering dataset, crucial for training the MU-LLaMA model

T2M-Gen Dataset Generation

We demonstrate the use of the MU-LLaMA model to generate music captions in various formats required for developing Text-to-music generation models

MusicQA Dataset Generation

We use MosaicML's MPT-7B-Chat Model to generate our MusicQA dataset.

Closed-ended Question Answer Pair Generation

Instructions:



- You are given a sentence describing an audio
- You will give answers from the audio to these questions based on the sentence
- Describe the music
- Describe the music in detail
- What do you hear in the audio
- What can be inferred from the audio
- The answers should be numbered

Open-ended Question Answer Pair Generation

Instructions:



- You are given a sentence describing an audio
- You will create 5 questions related to the audio based on the sentence along with answers
- The questions should be relating to things like tempo of the music, mood of the music, instruments used, inference, etc
- The question answers should be long form
- The question answers should be numbered
